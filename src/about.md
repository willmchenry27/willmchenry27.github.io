# About Me

I'm a mixed-methods researcher who bridges the worlds of **user experience research** and **international policy analysis**. My work at Amazon focuses on conducting consumer research studies that influence product and market decisions across Amazon Business, Prime, and Fire TV / Digital Advertising.

## Research Approach

I believe great research starts with the right questions. My background in foreign policy analysis — where ambiguity is the norm — trained me to:

- Design studies that uncover both the *what* and the *why*
- Combine quantitative and qualitative data into actionable insights
- Communicate complex findings to diverse stakeholders
- Challenge assumptions with evidence-based frameworks
